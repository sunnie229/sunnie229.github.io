About us

Our mission at HimCafe Brew is simple - bring to the world the highest quality coffee beans grown at the optimum altitude and climate, in the mountains of Nepal. Our organically grown coffee is an outcome of sustainable farming that empowers the largest farmer coffee cooperative in Nepal.

Himcafe Brew LLC.

HimCafe Brew LLC. is the official partner of Highland Organic Coffee Company which is the strategic outcome of Nepal Coffee Producers Association (NCPA) and is owned by both coffee entrepreneurs as well as farmers' group/co-operatives. Its unique partnership model is designed wherein Highland is joining hands with the coffee cooperatives that have a vision of producing coffee from 30 to 400 tons using efficient commercial farming practice, in turn, building necessary infrastructure at every levels to improve productivity to stay above the competition in the marketplace. Highland has been in operation since 1983 (formerly Nepal Coffee Company) and has been successfully exporting quality coffee to the international market.

Rebuilding after the earthquake

The deadly earthquakes of April 25, 2015 and May 12, 2015 devastated Nepal by killing more than 85,00 people and forcing millions more out of their homes. Many members of our coffee growing community were also affected. We believe that now is the time to help rebuild and strengthen the community by furthering employment oppertunites and providing a sustainable livelihood for the people in the community.

Sustainable and Organic Farming

Our Arabica coffee is planted in the shady foothills of Nepal, giving better quality beans while requiring less external inputs as compared to un-shaded cultivation method. Fodder and fruit trees are used as shade trees for their additional benefits. Inter-pollinating amont these crops keeps the soil healthy, and helps preserve biodiversity while reducing risk of crop failure. Irrigation, mulching, weeding, pruning, manuring, and pest management are applied in a very effective yet enduring manner throughout cultivation. Mulching and manuring is done by locally available plant materials and by-products from livestock, which are free of chemical fertilizers assuring a very high quality organic product.
